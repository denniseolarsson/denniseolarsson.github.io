I am a cognitive neuroscientist and, since January 2026, a [Humboldt](https://www.humboldt-foundation.de/en/connect/explore-the-humboldt-network/singleview/1247781/dr-dennis-larsson) Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the [Institute of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry](https://ikjp.cmsdemo.rz.uni-kiel.de/de/team), Christian-Albrechts-Universität zu Kiel, Germany. My research investigates how the developing brain processes information from the body.

I obtained my PhD in Experimental Psychology from the University of Sussex, United Kingdom, in 2022. Following this, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Neuroscience at the Karolinska Institute in Sweden.

My research interests include developmental neuroscience, brain–body interactions, and interoception.
