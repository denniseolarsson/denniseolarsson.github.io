I am a cognitive neuroscietist, and am since January 2026 a Humboldt postdoctoral research fellow at Institut für [Kinder- und Jugendpsychiatrie](https://ikjp.cmsdemo.rz.uni-kiel.de/de/team), Christian-Albrechts-Universität zu Kiel, Germany. My research investigates how the developing brain processes information from the body.

I obtained my PhD in experimental psychology at the University of Sussex in the United Kingdom in 2022, and following that I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Neuroscience at the Karolinska Institute in Sweden. 

My research interest are developmental neuroscience, brain-body interaction, and interoception.
