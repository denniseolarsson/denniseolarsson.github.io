I am a cognitive neuroscience researcher, and Humboldt postdoctoral fellow at Zentrum für Integrative Psychiatrie, Universitätsklinikum Schleswig-Holstein, Kiel, Germany. I obtained my PhD at the University of Sussex in the United Kingdom in 2022, and following that I was a postdoctoral researcher for two years at the Karolinska Institute in Sweden. 

My research interest are developmental neuroscience, brain-body interaction, and interoception.
